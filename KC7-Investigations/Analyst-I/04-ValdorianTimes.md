# Investigation 04 — Valdorian Times
**Platform:** KC7 Cyber
**Path:** Security Analyst I

---

## Scenario Summary
Investigated an email phishing attack in Valdoria
that uncovered a politically motivated influence
campaign. The investigation revealed how attackers
used phishing as an entry point for a larger
coordinated attack.

---

## What I Investigated
- Employee roles and organizational structure
- Email communications logs
- Computer process events
- Evidence of data exfiltration
- Data manipulation activities

---

## Attack Timeline
| Stage | What Happened |
|---|---|
| Initial Access | Phishing email sent to employees |
| Execution | Malicious content opened by victim |
| Exfiltration | Data stolen from organization |
| Impact | Data manipulated for influence campaign |
