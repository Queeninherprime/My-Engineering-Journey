## Key Topics Learned

### 1. What Is Defensive Security

- Responding to identified intrusions

- Detects malicious activity
- Analysts investigate alerts

### 3. Threat Intelligence

- Using intel to defend proactively

### 4. Digital Forensics & Incident Response

- Recovering from security incidents

- Collects logs from all systems
- Correlates events to find attacks
- Generates alerts for SOC analysts
- Directly connects to KC7 investigations
