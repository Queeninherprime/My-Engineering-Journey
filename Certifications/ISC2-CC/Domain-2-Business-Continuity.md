# Domain 2 — Business Continuity, DR & Incident Response


---

## 1. Business Continuity Planning (BCP)
- Ensures organization keeps operating during disruptions
- **BIA** — Business Impact Analysis (finds critical functions)
- **RTO** — Recovery Time Objective (how fast to recover)
- **RPO** — Recovery Point Objective (how much data loss is ok)
- **MTD** — Maximum Tolerable Downtime

---

## 2. Disaster Recovery Sites
| Site Type | Description | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| **Hot Site** | Fully operational, instant failover | Most expensive |
| **Warm Site** | Partially ready, needs some setup | Medium |
| **Cold Site** | Empty space, needs full setup | Cheapest |

---

## 3. Backup Types
| Type | What It Backs Up | Speed |
|---|---|---|
| **Full** | Everything | Slowest |
| **Incremental** | Changes since last backup | Fastest |
| **Differential** | Changes since last full backup | Medium |

---

## 4. Incident Response — 4 Phases
1. **Preparation** — Policies, tools, and training ready
2. **Detection & Analysis** — Identify what happened
3. **Containment, Eradication & Recovery** — Stop, remove, restore
4. **Post-Incident Activity** — Lessons learned report

---

## 5. Types of Incidents
- Malware infection
- Unauthorized access
- Data breach
- Denial of Service (DoS/DDoS)
- Insider threat
